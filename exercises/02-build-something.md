# 2. Build something small

Pick one. Or remix them. Or ignore the list and go off-script.

## Streamlit (web app with almost no effort)

- **Mood tracker**: rate your day 1-10, Claude draws a chart of your entries.
- **Flashcard quizzer**: give it a topic, get timed questions.
- **Awkward-silence question generator**: one button, one strange question.
- **Spotify-style "wrapped"** for your week's homework. Exaggerate everything.

## Website (HTML / JS)

- **One-page portfolio**: name, three interests, a photo, a link.
- **Decision wheel**: spin it to decide what you're doing tonight.
- **Typing speed test**: see how fast you are.

## Command line

- **Hangman**: the classic.
- **Word counter**: reads a file, tells you the top 10 words.
- **Personal cheat sheet**: a script that prints formulas / facts you need.

Tell Claude which one (or your own idea). Iterate until it feels right.
