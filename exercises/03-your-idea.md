# 3. Your own thing

Think of something you wish existed. A tool for your homework. A game for your group chat. A dumb experiment.

Describe it to Claude in one sentence. Let Claude ask you clarifying questions. Then go.

## Tips

- **Start small.** Get v1 running, then pile on features.
- **When it breaks**, copy the error to Claude and ask for a fix.
- **If it looks ugly**, say so. Claude can restyle.
- **If you get stuck**, ask Claude to suggest three directions you could take it.
- **Save anything you like** — ask Claude to help you push it to your own GitHub.
